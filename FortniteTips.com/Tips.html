TIPS AND TRICKS

TIPS

  1. Land close to where the first zone closes
  2. Upgrade weapons over time.
  3. listen for any footsteps around you that might be a player
  4. Keep a good range of weapons
  5. Crouching for good Accuracy, This will help with aim
  6. If you ever see a fight, and it has accurate players in it, dont push that fight then.
  7. Replenish health and shields immediately.
  8. Use the Kinetic Blade to rush opponents and take them by surprise
  9. Crouch and move while surrounded by opponents
  10. Hide until the final moments of a match
  11. Find the best landing spots
  12. Learn How To Build Properly
